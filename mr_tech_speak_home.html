MR Technically Speaking: Our Mission

Photo credit Marta Branco (pexels.com)

Welcome to MR Technically Speaking. I thank you for joining me on a learning journey through the world of emerging technology. While the technology will almost certainly be complicated, my plan will be simple. I will introduce you to a technology that has either been recently introduced or has been around for a while and is being applied in a new way or to solve a new problem. My introductions will only touch the surface, highlighting some potential pros and cons. Perhaps, something you read here will become something you want to know better, in which case, the next step will be yours. I will write these posts for the non-technical reader with links to sources which can help you dive deeper and learn more.Enjoy!
