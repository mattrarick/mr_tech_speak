MR Technically Speaking: About Us

My name is Matt Rarick. I live in the Phoenix, Arizona area with my family. I am a life-long learner, and I take great delight in sharing my knowledge and experiences with others. Technology is one of my favorite areas of interest. It can be so intimidating with its complexity, abstractness, and heavy use of jargonese. However, I think it is for those very reasons that it so gratifying to understand technical concepts well enough explain them to others. My hope is that something that I share with you will interest you enough to take a deeper dive into the subject matter. Then, you can share something new and interesting with me. Thank you for visiting my work. I hope to hear from you soon.
